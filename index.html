Understanding Market Sentiment

Market sentiment refers to the overall attitude of investors toward a particular security or financial market. It is the feeling or tone of a market, or its crowd psychology, as revealed through the activity and price movement of the securities traded in that market.

In crypto, sentiment is often driven by social media, news headlines, and influential figures. A positive tweet can send prices soaring, while news of a regulatory crackdown can cause a panic sell-off. Tools like the Fear & Greed Index attempt to quantify this sentiment, providing a snapshot of the market's emotional state.

Quick Tip: Secure Your Assets

Before you dive deep into trading, the most important step is securing your assets. The phrase "not your keys, not your coins" is a fundamental principle in the crypto space. Using a reputable hardware wallet to store your long-term holdings is far safer than keeping them on an exchange.
